Frequently Asked Questions

What do you want to know?

What are the age requirements for renting a car?

To rent a car, you must be between 21 and 75 years old. Drivers under 25 or over 75 may be subject to a young or senior driver surcharge.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and an additional form of identification, such as a passport or government-issued ID.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic insurance is usually included, covering liability and collision damage. Additional coverage options are available for enhanced protection.

How does the fuel policy work?

Most rentals require you to return the car with a full tank. Alternatively, pre-paid fuel options and other choices may be available.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation online or by contacting our customer service. Keep in mind that certain changes may incur fees.
